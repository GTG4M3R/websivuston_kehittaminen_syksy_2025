Services we provide

We provide a variety of car detailing services to ensure your needs are met.

All cars are snowfoamed to ensure excellent outcome.

We powerwash the cars using specialized methods.

We dry the cars carefully to ensure no streaks are left.

The packages we offer:

Silver - Basic detail ⭐

Quick basic clean for cars that mainly need exterior freshening up.

The package includes:

Exterior wash, wheel and tire cleaning,and exterior glass cleaning.

Pricing details
Gold - Full Interior & Exterior ⭐⭐

More complete package for cars that are clearly dirty inside and out.

The package includes:

Everything in Silver plus an interior clean (vacuumed mats and seats, cleaned and protected plastic surfaces), interior and exterior glass cleaning, tire shine, and a light wax protection on the exterior.

Pricing details
Platinum - Premium Protection ⭐⭐⭐

Full detail and protection for cars that you want to keep looking like new for as long as possible.

The package includes:

Everything in Gold plus paint decontamination (e.g. clay treatment or similar), machine polishing to reduce light scratches, ceramic or long‑lasting wax protection, extra protection for wheels and glass, and a deep clean and treatment of interior materials (such as fabric or leather care).
